About Me


I introduce myself, I am Gabriele Ventrice and I am a videogame programmer born and located in Italy. As a developer, I like to know everything related to my field of interest. I currently use the Unity game engine as my main platform.


The world of game dev aroused my curiosity for the first time when I was 13 years old, but before I started to become passionate about the actual development it had to wait until 2017, when, under different external stimuli in my life, I decided that I wanted to turn my passion into a gateway to a new world to explore.


I am currently attending the fifth year of a computer and telecommunications course at a high school and I am about to get my diploma in Computer Science. Through this course of study, I acquired the basics, then deepened on Java and C++, networking (I'm about to acquire a CISCO certification in this regard) and web development with HTML, CSS, PHP and MySQL.
